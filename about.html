About Us

Bold colours. Real quality. Fashion for every individual.

Our Story

Fashion Forward is an Indian online clothing brand founded with a simple mission — to make vibrant, premium-quality fashion accessible to everyone. We believe that what you wear is a form of self-expression, and we want to give you the most colourful, joyful palette to express yourself with.

What We Sell

We offer a curated collection of over 60 unique clothing styles — from flowing summer dresses and ethnic sarees to tailored suits, denim, knitwear, streetwear, and outerwear. Every piece is handpicked for its quality, colour, and craftsmanship. Our price range starts from ₹999 and goes up to ₹6,999, making premium fashion affordable.

Premium Quality, Every Time

We source only carefully selected fabrics — soft cottons, breathable linens, rich satins, and warm knits. Every item undergoes quality checks before being listed on our store. We are committed to delivering garments that look great, feel great, and last long.

Fast & Reliable Delivery

We process all orders within 1–2 business days and ship across India. Standard delivery takes 3–5 business days. We offer free shipping on all orders above ₹5,000. Every shipment comes with a tracking number so you always know where your parcel is.

Easy Returns & Refunds

Not happy with your order? No problem. We accept returns within 7 days of delivery. Refunds are processed within 5–7 business days to your original payment method. See our Cancellation & Refund Policy for full details.
