Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 27, 2026

1. Information We Collect

We collect personal information that you voluntarily provide to us when you register on the website, express an interest in obtaining information about us or our products, or when you contact us. This includes your name, email address, phone number, and billing/shipping addresses.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use personal information collected via our website for a variety of business purposes described below:

  • To fulfill and manage your orders.
  • To send administrative information to you.
  • To respond to your inquiries and offer support.

3. Will Your Information Be Shared?

We only share information with your consent, to comply with laws, to provide you with services (such as shipping providers and payment gateways like Razorpay/Cashfree), to protect your rights, or to fulfill business obligations.

4. Data Security

We have implemented appropriate technical and organizational security measures designed to protect the security of any personal information we process. All payment transactions are encrypted using secure socket layer technology (SSL) and stored with a AES-256 encryption.
